Soft & Chewy Banana Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 cup chocolate chips or chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add mashed bananas, egg, and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture. Stir in chocolate chips or nuts if using.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden.
  7. Let cool on a wire rack. Enjoy your banana cookies!
